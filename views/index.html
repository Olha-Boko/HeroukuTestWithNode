Innovative Cushioning System

We spent years obsessing the beads—finding the right size, shape and feel to deliver a soft,comfortable ride. Here’s how it works: Every time you take a step—or stride—10,000+ beadsshape around your foot to provide personalized cushioning and support, before responsively bouncing back to propel you forward.

Nike Joyride is designed to work for every runner, at every stage of their journey. It helps keep your legs and feet feeling fresh, so you’ll want to go again—whether yesterday was the longest run of your life or your first run ever.
